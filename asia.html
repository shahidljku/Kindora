Tian Shan, Kyrgyzstan

🗓️ Best Time: June - September (warm weather, accessible trails)
🏃 Activities: Hiking, horseback riding, yurt stays, eagle hunting
💰 Daily Budget: $30-50 (including accommodation & meals)
💡 Tips: Bring warm layers, hire local guides, respect nomadic customs
🌟 Highlights: Song-Kul Lake, Tash Rabat caravanserai, Jailoo experiences

Japan

🗓️ Best Time: March-May & September-November (cherry blossoms & autumn)
🏃 Activities: Temple visits, hot springs, bullet train rides, traditional dining
💰 Daily Budget: $80-150 (mid-range accommodation & meals)
💡 Tips: Learn basic phrases, carry cash, respect bowing customs
🌟 Highlights: Tokyo Skyline, Kyoto Temples, Mount Fuji, Cherry Blossoms

Altai Mountains

🗓️ Best Time: June - August (short summer season, snow-free passes)
🏃 Activities: Trekking, river rafting, horseback riding, wildlife watching
💰 Daily Budget: $25-40 (basic guesthouses & local meals)
💡 Tips: Pack warm gear, arrange permits, bring insect repellent
🌟 Highlights: Belukha Peak, Katun River, pristine lakes, nomadic culture

China

🗓️ Best Time: April-June & September-October (mild weather, clear skies)
🏃 Activities: Great Wall hiking, Forbidden City tours, Yangtze cruises
💰 Daily Budget: $50-100 (mid-range hotels & authentic dining)
💡 Tips: Get VPN for internet, learn basic Mandarin, carry translation app
🌟 Highlights: Great Wall, Forbidden City, Terracotta Warriors, Li River

Myanmar & Sri Lanka

🗓️ Best Time: November-March (dry season, cooler temperatures)
🏃 Activities: Temple exploration, tea plantation tours, wildlife safaris
💰 Daily Budget: $30-60 (guesthouses, local transportation)
💡 Tips: Respect religious sites, dress conservatively, try local cuisine
🌟 Highlights: Bagan temples, Sigiriya Rock, tea country, golden beaches

🏃 Activities: Diving, snorkeling, volcano hiking, beach relaxation
💰 Daily Budget: $25-50 (beachside bungalows, fresh seafood)
💡 Tips: Island hop carefully, respect marine life, try local street food
🌟 Highlights: Bali temples, Palawan lagoons, Komodo dragons, coral reefs

🏃 Activities: Palace tours, K-pop experiences, hiking, spa visits

🌟 Highlights: Seoul palaces, Jeju Island, Busan beaches, DMZ tours

Mongolia

🗓️ Best Time: June-August (warm summer, Naadam Festival season)
🏃 Activities: Horseback riding, ger stays, desert camping, eagle hunting
💰 Daily Budget: $40-70 (ger camps, guided tours, traditional meals)
💡 Tips: Respect nomadic traditions, pack warm clothes, hire local guides
🌟 Highlights: Gobi Desert, wild horses, Ulaanbaatar, nomadic culture

💰 Daily Budget: $20-50 (heritage hotels, authentic cuisine, rickshaw rides)
💡 Tips: Stay hydrated, respect religious customs, bargain at markets
🌟 Highlights: Taj Mahal, Golden Triangle, Kerala backwaters, Rajasthan palaces

🏃 Activities: Trekking, mountaineering, temple visits, meditation retreats
